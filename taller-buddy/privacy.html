Taller Buddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Taller Buddy collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. Taller Buddy is a growth habit-tracking application that helps you establish daily habits — such as sleep, exercise, and nutrition — that support healthy growth, so you can reach the higher end of your genetic height range.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information (email address) when you sign up
  • Profile data: birth date, gender, height measurements, and parent heights
  • Daily habit tracking data (sleep, exercise, nutrition, and other growth-related habits)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Feedback and support communications when you contact us directly

2.2 Automatically collected information

  • Device information such as device type and operating system version
  • App usage analytics such as features used and session duration
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • App Store transaction information handled through RevenueCat

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or camera access
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, face scans, etc.)

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide growth tracking, height prediction, and percentile analysis
  • Generate personalized growth insights and recommendations
  • Process subscription payments through app store billing systems
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve performance, reliability, and product quality
  • Send important service-related notifications where appropriate

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Third-party services

  • Supabase: secure cloud database for account and growth data storage, with row-level security
  • RevenueCat: subscription management and payment processing
  • App Store and Google Play: distribution and billing

4.2 We do not

  • Sell personal information to third parties
  • Share data for third-party advertising
  • Allow third parties to use app data for unrelated marketing

5. Data Security

  • We use reasonable measures to protect information in our systems
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest where applicable
  • Row-level security ensures users can only access their own data
  • No method of transmission or storage is completely secure

6. Data Retention

  • We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service
  • You can request deletion of personal information by contacting us
  • Account deletion removes all associated child profiles and growth data
  • Some records may be retained where required for legal or operational reasons

7. Your Rights

  • Access: request access to personal information
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out: unsubscribe from optional communications

8. Children's Privacy

  • The app is intended for users aged 4 and above
  • Users under 13 should use the app with parental supervision
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13 without parental consent
  • A parent or guardian can review, modify, or request deletion of data at any time

9. International Users

  • Information may be processed in countries other than your own
  • Appropriate safeguards are used where applicable

10. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • Material changes may be communicated through the app or related channels

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact info@dorapockettech.com or use the support resources available through the app and support page.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance to provide the app and its services
  • Legitimate interests in improving and maintaining the product
  • Consent where consent is specifically requested

By using Taller Buddy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Questions about privacy can be sent to info@dorapockettech.com.
